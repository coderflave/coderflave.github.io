Rockaway Beach

A very popular free beach in the summer. Its the only legal surfing beach in NYC. Facilities include basketball and volley ball courts. It even has a baseball field available. To find out more click here.

Arthur Ashe Stadium

Home of the US Open. What many people may not know is that the qualifying tournament that runs the week before the actual open is free to the public. Arther Ashe kids day is another free day. Once inside you can join tennis clinics and see live music for free. More info can be found here.

NYC Parks

The NYC parks department has multiple recreational centers thoughout the boroughs of the city. The centers offer multiple activities and classes which include but are not limited to swimming lessons, movie screenings, fitness classes, and computer classes. Click here for more info.
