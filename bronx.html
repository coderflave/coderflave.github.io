New York Botanical Garden

While there is an admissions fee to enter the gardens on most days, you can go for free all day on Wednesday and between 10am and 12pm on Saturday. A visit is well worth the price regardless of the price. Besides the beautiful gardens you also go to events happening throughout the week including live performers, outdoor exhibits, and a farmers market. Find out more here.

Van Cortdland Park

This park is part of the NYC park department which means that like other locations there is a pool and other activies offered. This park stands out from the others due to the 6 tennis courts available to the public. There are also free pony rides available to children. Find out more info here.
